Ogden Musical Theater Values

Excellence

We are a non-equity theatre showcasing local talent, with team committed to top-quality.

Inclusion

We offer inclusive programs that reflect Ogden’s diversity and aim to make a lasting community impact.

Education

We value education and support the growth of artists of all ages in our company.

Kids Act Up!

2024 Camp Photo

2025 Kids Act Up! - June 16th - June 27th

KIDS ages 8 to 14 years old- Ogden Musical Theatre presents a fun and intense theatre production workshop taught by Director Mark Daniels. The 10-day camp will conclude with a student production of the stage musical listed below. Throughout the workshop we will cover principles of singing, acting, and choreography. It will include some acting exercises and improvisation games. This camp will give the kids valuable audition and production experience so they can feel confident in extending their experience to community and school productions.

Camp this year will consist of two classes:

  1. DISCOVERY CLASS - For students entering school grades 3-5 this fall.
  2. VENTURE CLASS - For students entering school grades 6-9 this fall.

These two classes will overlap and will each be taught by some excited and talented teachers, some that have worked for KAU before and some new faces we are bringing on board as we expand the opportunities.
